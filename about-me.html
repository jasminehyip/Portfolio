INTRODUCTION

My name is Jasmine and I'm based in Toronto, Ontario. I am currently a 2nd year Global Business and Digital Arts Student at the University of Waterloo; studying a combination of technology, business, and UX/UI Design.

December 2025 - Cultural Exchange to Tohoku University in Sendai, Japan.

TO BRIDGING COMMUNITIES TOGETHER

While it is still early in my career, I aspire to work in marketing and branding between Asia and North America. One of my passions that I pursue in my free time is learning languages, and something I noticed about design is that the styles and consumer preferences between these demographics are completely different.

This means that design and word choice must adapt for the message to reach consumers! I am not yet fluent or an expert, but I look forward to growing in my craft little by little, and experiencing the best of both worlds when it comes to design!

December 2025 - Cultural Exchange to Tohoku University in Sendai, Japan.

Since 2023, I've had a passion for language learning because of how deeply culture and values are connected together through the language we use every day. Through volunteering at the General English Program at Renison (GEAR) Program at Renison University College on the UWaterloo campus, it has been amazing to meet so many people from different backgrounds. Learning about their experiences and seeing how different we all view the world is one of the many reasons why I love about connecting with people from different countries!
