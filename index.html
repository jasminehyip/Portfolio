THIS WEEK'S FEATURE

Ribbon-themed posters I created by generating elements using Adobe Firefly and arranging them using Illustrator and Photoshop! This was a poster I completed out of personal interest. I've always loved the colour pink and ribbons, so I decided to combine them together and practice utilising AI tools.

I also experimented with text and blending modes where elements overlap, and I think that was my favourite part! Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and look forward to next week's feature. ^-^
